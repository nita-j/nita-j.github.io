Spinach with Tuna and Avocado Recipe

For seared tuna:

1.Rub the tuna with black sesame seeds, white sesame seeds and rosemary.
2.Wrap the tuna in a cling film and refrigerate for 5-10 minutes.
3.Remove the cling film and sear the tuna on each side in some olive oil.
4.To Serve: Serve with baby spinach with avocado.

For spinach with avocado:

1.Prick the red chillies, rub olive oil and roast them.
2.When the skin of chillies is charred, put them in cold water. Remove the skin and seeds.
3.Cut the chillies in small pieces.
4.For dressing add extra virgin olive oil, groundnut oil, salt, lemon juice and the chillies in a bowl.
5.Toss the baby spinach and scooped flesh of avocado. Add the chilli dressing.
6.To Serve: Garnish with pumpkin seeds.
